SKILL BOOKS
  • AIR
      Air Blast - blast air from the limbs.
      Acceleration - uses wind as propeller to accelerate in any directions.
      Wind shield - generates a wind sphere to negate attacks.
      Tempest Raze - a special attack controlling the weather.
  • EARTH
      Rock Punch - covers hands with rocks to amplify combat damage.
      Earth Totem - summons a totem that constatly slows down enemy.
      Mud Slide - turns earth to mud and increases mobility.
      Tectonic Destruction - generates a strong earthquake destroying a certain radius.
  • FIRE
      Flame Shot - shots concentrated flame from limbs.
      Fire Wall - generates wall of fire to block attackes.
      Heat Jet - uses fire as force to amplify aerial movement.
      Infernal Lightning - concentrates energy into dark lightning to be used as weapon.
  • WATER
      Ice Splinter - turns water into ice to be used as spears.
      Water Pillar - creates water pillar to be used as a trap or defense.
      Glacial Wings - sprout ice wings from the back for aerial movement.
      Blood Fest - locate and weaken everyone in certain radius using their blood.
